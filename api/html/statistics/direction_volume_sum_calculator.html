direction_volume_sum_calculator¶

class ctxalgolib.statistics.direction_volume_sum_calculator.DirectionVolumeSumCalculator(instrument_id, start_output_date=None)¶

Bases: ctxalgolib.statistics.abstract_statistics_calculator.AbstractStatisticsCalculator

This calculator outputs two statistics: the sum over volumes for buy direction and sell direction, this means we sum over the volume column on rows whose direction column is 0 (for buy), and we sum over the volume column on rows whose direction column is 1 (for sell).
